About SpaceHorse

SpaceHorse is a free image placeholder service created by the Lion team (Hayk, Yashar, & Joshua) for #HackTheDot. You can use it right away -- simply point your images to the service's URL:

http://placehorse.ninja/100x100

The images are automatically resized to match the dimensions you specify. SpaceHorse is free, open source, and you can use it for any purpose, personal or commercial.
